Three-Child Family: Mute

China's three-child birth policy has a lot of opposition from public opinion. According to a survey, the quality of the parent-child relationship in a three-child family affects the development of the three-child family. I designed a specific storybook about the parent-child relationship of a Chinese three-child family. Parents issue an instruction in order to reduce the cost of education, and the environment that is easy to cause emotional violence to children leads to alienation of the parent-child relationship.

Help parents reflect on their children's needs, abandon toxic education after independent thinking, and accompany their children to grow together. Help those who have three children in the future understand that this is a prudent decision, learn to be a positive parent-child balance, and iterate the social parent-child alienation problem.

I chose a group of traditional Chinese herbs that balance the relationship, Pinellia and Ginger. The poison taught by mother is Pinellia, and the antidote is mother's tears and ginger. People will become mute after eating Pinellia and ginger can detoxify. Partridges and Bird Gods are designed, and Chinese people ate partridges to nourish their bodies in ancient times. In 2021, the partridge is listed as a globally endangered species by IUCN. The partridge is naturally fond of eating Pinellia, Bird God opened a Pinellia restaurant to help the partridge eat Pinellia to protect itself.

In the real world, the mother's children have become dolls, and the Bird god sweetly coaxes the child to keep the child to help her with errands. It depicts that parents ignore the needs of their children in order to achieve their goals. The composition of children's emotional management mechanism originates from parents. The behavior of the parents also did not find the problem and continued because they had the same situation when they were children.
