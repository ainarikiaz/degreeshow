Transmigration des Âmes Errantes

'Transmigration des Âmes Errantes' is a world building narrative based on my transgender experience. It is a space that has granted me the ability to immerse myself within the psyche of my younger self, cherry picking the vital moments of my trans awakening and illustrating them into a story as a way for me to transform past experiences of dysphoria and experiment with the mental and physical forms in which dysphoria can manifest.

Part one symbolises the shattering of my delusion and the awakening to a long awaited trans truth, focussing on how it feels to be 'trapped' under the influence of cisnormative ways of seeing. I wanted to symbolise just how disingenuous this way of seeing was for me through mediums of storytelling, illustration, world building and character design to paint a more representative narrative of trans experiences.
