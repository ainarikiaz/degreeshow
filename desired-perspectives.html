Desired Perspectives: The Scalascope and Chocolate Concept Communities

An exploration of access to different vantage points and the hostility with these vistas. How do we democratise these perspectives and encapsulate a landscape of the city? As a site of social turmoil for the rights to views, I'm using the Tate Modern as a case study. I want to actualise public aspirations through two contradicting perspectives (God's eye view v ant view); the Scalascope rescales the natural stereoscopic scale of our eyes to meet and subvert the city planning scale, and ingesting the scaled down concept models of ourselves is to further the dialogue between person and city through minute interactions.
