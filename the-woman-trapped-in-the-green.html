"The woman trapped in the green"

My project explores OCD. I found that the best time to prevent OCD is between the ages of eight and fourteen. So I made a children's picture book and I developed the story into postcards and an interactive installation. I hope to encourage children to face their abnormal psychology actively, and the postcards can be a tool of communication between children and their parents, so that parents can timely understand their children's needs and psychological changes.

The story focuses on OCD about colour, telling the story of a woman trapped in green. I made a set which can look at the world in the perception of patient with OCD. I hope that there will be less discrimination against abnormal behavior in society, and people can understand and respect eachother.
