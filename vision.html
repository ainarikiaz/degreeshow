VISION

In recent years, social media and tech companies have grown to become monopolies, and these monopolies are providing centralised platforms that we have grown to depend on. As users, we have no infrastructure to control what data these companies have on us, how they use the data, what they share and with whom. There are virtually no laws protecting our rights to data. These monopolies have been allowed to mould the future with their malignant use of technology. How can we undo the power dynamics that have been created, when we continue to depend on these platforms?

VISION seeks to solve this problem. VISION is a platform that enables you to take back ownership of your personal data and license it to companies who wish to use it. This is done by utilizing NFT Technology, which enables digital ownership of data. This technology also provides guaranteed security and verification using biometrics. We want to educate the world about the importance and value of a person's data, and we want to showcase the beauty of data by creating our interpretation of what data looks like. We call this the Digital Soul NFT, and this graphic is designed to be interactive so that when your data is used or updated, the dynamic graphic changes. It also comes with the Holo-Pyramid, which projects a hologram of your data within the Holo-Pyramid and by doing this, we make the intangible, tangible.

We believe that data is an archive of oneself, and no company or entity should own it. The user should, and it's time we take it back.
